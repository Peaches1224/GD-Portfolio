Adobe Photoshop is a powerful and versatile software application used for image editing, graphic design, and digital art. It is widely used by photographers, designers, artists, and other creative professionals to create and manipulate images.

Key Features: Image Editing: Crop, resize, and adjust images for better composition and quality. Layer-Based Editing: Work with multiple layers to edit images non-destructively and maintain flexibility in design. Selection Tools: Use tools like the Magic Wand, Lasso, and Quick Selection to isolate parts of an image for detailed editing. Adjustment Layers: Apply adjustments such as brightness, contrast, hue, and saturation in a way that can be easily modified. Filters and Effects: Apply a wide range of filters and effects to enhance or stylize images. Retouching Tools: Use tools like the Clone Stamp, Healing Brush, and Spot Healing Brush to remove blemishes and imperfections. Text and Shape Tools: Add and style text and shapes to create graphic elements. Advanced Features: Utilize more complex tools and features such as masks, blend modes, smart objects, and vector shapes.
